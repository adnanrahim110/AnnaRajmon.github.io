About the Author
Anna Rajmon

Anna, originally from the Czech Republic, has experienced a life full of challenging moments. Despite her education and achieving high professional positions, she eventually found herself in an unexpected situation where she decided to enter the world of prostitution to provide for her daughter. This dramatic turn in her life led her to write a book that could inspire people worldwide with her story. She aims to illuminate a world many are curious about, but few truly understand. Through her story, she also seeks to caution girls who are considering entering the world of escorting and to highlight the shameful behaviour and dangers associated with this lifestyle. Anna hopes that her book will bring awareness and change and that it can help those who find themselves in similar situations to the one she experienced.

About “ELIS Irish callgirl” By Anna Rajmon

Anna Rajmon’s book, “Based On A True Story,” is a powerful and inspiring tale of resilience, courage, and the unwavering strength of the human spirit. Through her tumultuous journey of navigating a toxic relationship, motherhood, and the challenges she faced, Anna's story serves as a beacon of hope for readers facing their own struggles.From a difficult childhood to a toxic marriage, Anna’s narrative is a testament to the power of perseverance in the face of adversity.
Despite the hardships she endured, she found solace in her daughter and her dog, creating a support system that carried her through the darkest of times. Her journey of defiance against a controlling husband marks a turning point in her life, showcasing her unwavering determination to break free from toxic relationships and reclaim her own happiness.
